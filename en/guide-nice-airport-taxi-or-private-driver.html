Nice Airport: taxi or private driver?

When you land at Nice Côte d’Azur Airport, you usually have several options: public transport, regular taxi or private driver. This guide focuses on the difference between taxi and private driver.

How a taxi works at Nice Airport

  • Official taxi ranks are located just outside the terminals.
  • Fares are metered and can vary depending on traffic and route.
  • There may be a queue at peak arrival times.

How a private driver service works

  • Your trip is requested in advance, usually by message or booking form.
  • You receive an estimated price before confirming.
  • The driver waits for you at the agreed meeting point with a name sign.

Which option should you choose?

If you are travelling light and speak some French, a taxi can be a simple last-minute option. If you prefer a more personalised service, with prices shared in advance and direct contact with the driver, a private driver is often more comfortable.

To learn more about private transfers, you can check the page Nice Airport → Cannes transfer or send a request through the French booking form.
