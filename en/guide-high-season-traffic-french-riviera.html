High season traffic on the French Riviera

During summer and major holiday periods, traffic and parking can become challenging on the French Riviera. This guide gives a few simple ideas to reduce stress.

Choosing your main travel times

  • Try to avoid peak weekend times for airport transfers and check-ins.
  • Plan extra time before important appointments or events.
  • Consider early morning or later evening departures when possible.

Parking in busy areas

Some coastal towns and resorts have limited parking in high season. If you rent a car, check your accommodation’s parking options and nearby public car parks.

When a private driver can be helpful

For key transfers (airport, important dinners, events, hilltop villages), using a private driver may be simpler than driving yourself. You avoid searching for parking and can focus on your stay.

To organise your main transfers, you can describe your dates and key journeys through the booking form.
