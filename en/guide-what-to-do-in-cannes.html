What to do in Cannes

Cannes is known for its festivals, luxury hotels and seafront. Here are a few ideas to enjoy the town without a complicated schedule.

Walk along the Croisette

  • Start with a walk along the Croisette between the beaches and the hotels.
  • Take time for a coffee or lunch with a sea view.
  • Explore the side streets around the main shopping area.

Discover the old town and the harbour

  • Climb up to the old town for a view over the bay.
  • Walk around the harbour and watch the boats.
  • Choose a simple restaurant in the old streets or near the harbour.

Small excursions from Cannes

From Cannes, it is easy to visit nearby places such as Antibes, hilltop villages or coastal towns. You can use trains, buses or a private driver depending on your schedule.
