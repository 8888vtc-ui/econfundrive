How to book

Simple booking process

To request a transfer, please fill in the French booking form (easy to understand) with:

  • Pick‑up address (or airport / train station) and drop‑off address.
  • Date, time and flight or train number if applicable.
  • Number of passengers and pieces of luggage.

In most cases, transfers must be booked at least 4 hours before pick‑up time. For very last‑minute requests, you can call the driver or use the WhatsApp button on the website to check if a slot is still available (no guarantee).

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I book?

Most transfers are booked at least 4 hours before pick‑up time. During busy periods such as festivals, congresses or summer holidays, it is recommended to book as early as possible to secure a slot.

Which areas do you cover on the French Riviera?

The main areas are Nice, Cannes, Antibes, Monaco and the Gulf of Saint‑Tropez (Fréjus, Sainte‑Maxime, Saint‑Tropez and surrounding villages). Some longer trips can be organised on request, depending on the schedule and driving time.

Which languages do you speak?

The service is available in English and French, with regular experience welcoming international guests who may not speak French or know the region well.

Do I need to pay online?

No online payment is requested on the website. You first receive an indicative quote and confirmation of availability, then the booking is confirmed once you agree on the conditions and payment method directly with the driver.

How can I get a quick answer?

The fastest way is usually to send a short message via WhatsApp or to call. The driver can then confirm availability and an approximate price based on your pick‑up location, destination, date, time and number of passengers.
