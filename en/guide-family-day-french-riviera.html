Family guide

Family day out on the French Riviera

With a bit of planning, the French Riviera can be very pleasant with children. The idea is to keep the day simple and avoid too many changes of place.

Combining beach and easy walk

  • Choose a beach that is easy to access with children and bags.
  • Keep some time for a short walk in a nearby town or village.
  • Plan simple meals and enough breaks during the day.

Thinking in terms of rhythm, not in terms of places

Instead of trying to see as many places as possible, it is often better to think about the rhythm of the day: when the children are usually full of energy, when they need rest, and how long they can stay in the car.

How a private driver can help families

  • Child seats can usually be arranged on request.
  • You do not have to carry luggage or pushchairs on public transport.
  • You can shorten or extend the day depending on how everyone feels.

When you send your request through the booking form, you can mention the age of the children and your preferred type of day.
