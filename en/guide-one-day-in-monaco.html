One day in Monaco from the French Riviera

Monaco is a classic day trip from Nice, Antibes or Cannes. This guide suggests a simple way to spend a day there, without turning it into a race.

Morning: old town and palace area

  • Start with the old town and the palace area.
  • Walk through the small streets and visit a few viewpoints.
  • Take a break in a café before heading down again.

Afternoon: Monte-Carlo and harbour

  • Walk around Monte-Carlo and the gardens near the casino.
  • Go down towards the harbour to see the yachts and the bay.
  • Leave some time for a drink or an early dinner before your return.

How to get to Monaco

You can reach Monaco by train, bus or car from many towns on the French Riviera. A private driver is useful if you want to combine Monaco with photo stops on the scenic roads or a visit to a hilltop village.

If you prefer a flexible schedule, you can mention your plan in the booking form and ask for a quote including Monaco and a few stops along the way.
