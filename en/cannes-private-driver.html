FAQ – private driver in Cannes

How early should I book during festivals and congresses?

During major events such as the Cannes Film Festival, MIPIM or Cannes Lions, it is strongly recommended to book as early as possible (often several weeks in advance) to secure vehicles and drivers.

Can you coordinate several vehicles for my group?

Yes, it is possible to coordinate several cars or vans – and sometimes minibuses via trusted partners – for your teams, clients or guests, provided that the request is made early enough.

Which airports do you work with for Cannes?

The main airports are Nice Côte d’Azur (NCE) and Cannes‑Mandelieu, with transfers to hotels, villas and the Palais des Festivals, as well as return journeys at the end of your stay or event.

Do you provide late‑night transfers in Cannes?

Yes, late‑night or very early morning transfers can be organised, especially during festivals and private events, if they are booked sufficiently in advance.

Is the service suitable for international guests?

Yes, the service is adapted to international guests attending festivals, congresses or private events in Cannes, with clear communication in English and an understanding of the specific constraints linked to tight schedules.
