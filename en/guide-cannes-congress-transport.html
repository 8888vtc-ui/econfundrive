Getting around during Cannes congresses

During major congresses, such as MIPIM or Cannes Lions, it is important to treat transport as part of your overall organisation, not as an afterthought.

Airport transfers for speakers and teams

For key speakers or teams arriving at Nice Airport, you can plan individual or shared transfers. Share flight details, hotel addresses and your internal contact person with your driver.

Daily shuttles between hotel and Palais

  • Define your busiest time slots in the morning and evening.
  • Decide whether you need a car on stand-by or only punctual transfers.
  • Anticipate late returns after dinners or networking events.

Sharing a clear brief

When requesting a quote, a short but precise brief (dates, number of people, type of use) helps build a realistic offer. You can send these details using the booking form.
