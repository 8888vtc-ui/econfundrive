Nice guide

How to spend 1 to 3 days in Nice

Nice is easy to explore in a few days. Here is a simple way to organise a short stay, without trying to see everything at once.

Day 1: old town and seaside

  • Walk through the old town and its narrow streets.
  • Enjoy the local market and cafés.
  • Finish the day with a walk along the Promenade des Anglais.

Day 2: museums and viewpoints

On your second day, you may want to:

  • visit one or two museums depending on your interests;
  • take a walk on one of the hills around Nice for a sea view;
  • go out for dinner in the old town or by the sea.

You can use public transport, taxis or a private driver for the trips that are further away from the centre.

Day 3: easy day trips

If you stay three days, consider a simple day trip to a nearby town or village. For example: Antibes, a hilltop village, or a short visit to Monaco.

A private driver can be useful if you prefer a flexible schedule and several stops in the same day. You can mention this in the booking form.
