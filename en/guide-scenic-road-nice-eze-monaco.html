Scenic drive guide

Scenic road between Nice, Èze and Monaco

The coastline between Nice and Monaco can be explored using several roads at different heights, each with its own atmosphere and viewpoints.

Different types of roads

  • Lower coastal road: close to the sea and beaches.
  • Middle road: a mix of sea views and village access.
  • Higher road: wider views over the coast and the hills.

Ideas for simple stops

  • Short photo stops at safe viewpoints.
  • A walk through a village on the way.
  • A coffee or drink with a view before continuing.

Driving or using a private driver

You can explore this area with a rental car, public transport or a private driver. If you prefer to focus on the views and stops, a driver can take care of the route and parking.

To book such a drive, you can note your preferred type of road and number of hours in the booking form.
