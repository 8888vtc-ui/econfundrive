Long-distance guide

Planning a long-distance transfer from the French Riviera

Sometimes a train or a flight is not the most convenient option. A private long-distance transfer can be a good alternative for families, business travellers or small groups.

When does a long-distance transfer make sense?

  • When you travel with several people and a lot of luggage.
  • When your destination is not well connected by train or plane.
  • When you prefer a direct journey from door to door.

Information to share when you request a quote

  • Exact pick-up and drop-off addresses.
  • Date, approximate time and possible flexibility.
  • Number of passengers and luggage.
  • Any special needs (child seats, extra stops, return trip).

Making the journey more comfortable

For long-distance trips, it is usually better to plan a few short breaks. Let your driver know if you prefer to stop in a particular town or service area.

To describe your project, you can use the booking form and mention that you are interested in a long-distance transfer from the French Riviera.
