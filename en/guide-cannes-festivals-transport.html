Cannes guide

Getting around during Cannes festivals

During the Cannes Film Festival, MIPIM, Cannes Lions and other major events, the town becomes very busy. Here are a few simple ideas to keep your transport under control.

Plan your airport transfers

Most international guests arrive through Nice Airport. If you are travelling in a group or have a tight schedule, plan your transfers in advance:

  • share your flight number and hotel address with your driver;
  • allow extra time for traffic between the airport and Cannes;
  • consider grouped transfers for teams or guests.

Moving between hotel, Palais and evening venues

During festivals, a typical day often includes meetings, screenings, dinners and parties. Using a private driver can help you:

  • avoid waiting for taxis at busy times;
  • secure pick-up and drop-off times in advance;
  • offer a reliable service to your guests or clients.

Coordinating your schedule

When you send your request, try to include your main time slots: arrival and departure, important meetings, evening events. This makes it easier to suggest a realistic transport plan.
