Hilltop villages of the French Riviera in one day

The French Riviera is not only about the sea. Several hilltop villages offer great views and quiet streets, just a short drive from the coast.

Examples of villages to combine

  • One or two popular villages for a first visit.
  • Another stop mainly for the view and a short walk.
  • A final stop for a drink or simple meal.

Why a private driver can help

  • You do not have to focus on narrow or winding roads.
  • You can adjust the time spent in each village.
  • You avoid searching for parking spaces several times in the same day.

If you would like to organise this kind of day, you can describe your idea in the booking form and mention the villages or type of scenery you would like to see.
